Oscar Fish

Oscars are large sized fish, typically to the length of 35 cms. The required tank size for trouble-free Oscar fish care should have at least a space of 30 gallons.

  • Scientific Name: Oscar Fish
  • Average Length: 35 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Tropical area

They do require a clean and warm water within the temperature range of 74°C to 81°C. Not keeping them in required warm temperatures can upset their immune system and invite many diseases.
